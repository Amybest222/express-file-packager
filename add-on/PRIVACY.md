# Privacy Notice

Express File Packager does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

## Data Collection

- No account data is collected.
- No analytics or tracking data is collected.
- No document content is sent to external servers by this add-on.

## Data Processing

All processing happens locally within Adobe Express during export.

When you create a package, the add-on generates only the files you request, such as:

- PNG
- JPG
- PDF
- Optional `fonts.txt`

These files are downloaded to your device.

## Third-Party Services

Express File Packager does not require third-party services for export processing.
